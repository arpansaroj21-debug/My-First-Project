About Viksit Bharat

Viksit Bharat represents India's ambition to become a fully developed nation by 2047. It focuses on technological advancement, economic strength, social inclusion, world-class infrastructure and sustainable development for future generations.

Sustainable Development

Clean energy, environmental balance and long-term ecological growth.
